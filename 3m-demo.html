8 · Recommended Sales Plays
1
Filtrete CTV-to-Direct Upgrade
Expansion

3M's Filtrete brand is already active on TWC properties via Amazon Q4 wins (Allergy, Very Hot, Very Cold) and spending $10.6M in CTV. Convert that programmatic/Amazon relationship into a direct, premium CTV and weather-triggered package before the spring allergy and summer heat planning window opens.

  • Reference the three Q4 2025 Filtrete Amazon wins as the warm entry point. Email or call Colle McVoy: "We delivered on all three Filtrete Q4 buys — Allergy, Very Hot, and Very Cold. I want to show you how a direct TWC deal for Q2 spring allergy could outperform the Amazon programmatic path on CPM efficiency and audience precision."
  • Surface the Q2 budget structure. "For Filtrete's spring allergy push, is the Q2 budget currently allocated to the Amazon programmatic line, or is there a separate direct-buy pool at Colle McVoy we should be talking to?"
  • Position TWC's weather-triggered CTV vs. Amazon programmatic. Real-time pollen index and temperature targeting means Filtrete ads serve exactly when consumers are symptomatic — not just when a keyword fires. Bundle the two lost TWC-Interactive banner opps into the CTV pitch to close the display gap simultaneously.
  • Quantify the shift. 3M spends $10.6M in CTV. A 5% shift from programmatic to direct TWC = ~$530K in incremental direct revenue. Weather-triggered delivery typically reduces wasted impressions 20–30% vs. flat CPM buys.
  • Ask for a 30-minute Q2 planning call. "Can we get 30 minutes before end of [month] to walk through a Filtrete spring allergy weather-trigger proposal? I'll bring a comp analysis of the Amazon programmatic CPMs vs. our direct CTV rate card."
2
Displace Paid Social with TWC Premium Digital
Displacement

3M is allocating $24M of its $55M digital budget to Paid Social — the single largest digital line. With two consecutive TWC-Interactive losses and no visible media DM, route through Colle McVoy to reframe a portion of that Paid Social spend toward TWC's premium digital and online video inventory.

  • Lead with the spend data. "3M's Paid Social line is running around $24M — the largest single digital channel. Given brand-safety and measurement scrutiny on social platforms right now, I'd like to share how TWC's premium digital environment has been performing for comparable consumer brands."
  • Ask about the two lost TWC-Interactive proposals. "On the Q4'25 and Q1'26 proposals that didn't close — was the barrier budget timing, format fit, or a preference to consolidate on social? Understanding that helps me come back with something that actually fits the plan."
  • Propose a brand-safe premium digital package. Online Video + Internet Display + weather-contextual placements — as a measurable alternative to a portion of the $24M Paid Social allocation, specifically for 3M's Consumer or Safety segments.
  • Quantify the test. A $500K–$1M reallocation to TWC Online Video + Display = a 14–28% expansion of 3M's existing TWC digital footprint and gives Colle McVoy a clean A/B data set to justify further shifts at the next planning cycle.
  • Offer a competitive displacement brief. "I can put together a one-page comparison of TWC premium digital vs. top social platforms on brand-safety, viewability, and contextual relevance for 3M's consumer segments. I can have it to you within five business days — useful ahead of your next internal planning review?"
Use these as seller moves, not just research notes. When a seasonal hook is present, get in front of the account before the demand window starts.
